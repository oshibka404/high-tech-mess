High Tech Mess is a new event series that aims to bring together the best of the Amsterdam underground. We are a collective of artists, musicians, and designers who are passionate about creating unique experiences.

Our first event will be held at murmur, a new venue in Amsterdam Noord. We will be showcasing a variety of local talent, including live music, DJs, and visual artists.

We hope to see you there!
